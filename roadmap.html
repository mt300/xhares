Roadmap

Phase 1: November 3rd, 2024

  • Official launch of the collection of 10,000 hare NFTs.
  • Promotion through social media.
  • Partnerships with influencers and communities to maximize reach.

Phase 2: November 4th, 2024

  • Launch of the official collection website.

Phase 3: January 2025

  • Start of the minting of fungible tokens corresponding to the items included in the NFT metadata.
  • Launch of the rewards program for NFT holders.
  • Creation of community guide on how to utilize fungible tokens.

Phase 4: To be determined in 2025

  • Introduction of minting for properties within the xSpectarverse.
  • Implementation of features for buying, selling, and renting digital properties.
  • Marketing campaigns and events to promote the new functionality.
